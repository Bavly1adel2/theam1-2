How to position your AI SaaS for clarity

A demo blog post layout with readable typography, semantic markup, and strong hierarchy.

NeuroAI ships with a clean editorial layout built on Tailwind Typography. Use this page as a starter for articles, release notes, and documentation.

Make the value obvious

Prefer short sections, specific benefits, and concrete proof. Use stats, testimonials, and comparison blocks to remove friction.

Show the product early

Your hero area should show a clear outcome, a primary CTA, and a preview of the UI — especially if you're selling an AI tool.

“Clear positioning is a conversion multiplier — it reduces cognitive load and increases trust.”

Use consistent components

Keep cards, buttons, and form inputs consistent across marketing, directory, and dashboard pages. This template is designed around reusable partials to make that easy.
